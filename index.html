YOUR COMPETITORS ARE IN AI ANSWERS. YOU'RE NOT.

How does your business perform when someone asks AI tools for your product or service?

When a buyer asks ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI who to hire for your service, AI generates an answer and recommends specific businesses by name. Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) is the process of making sure your business is one of them. That's what LUMO does.
