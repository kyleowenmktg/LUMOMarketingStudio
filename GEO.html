What is GEO?

GEO ensures your brand and content are optimized to appear in AI-powered search engines, chatbots, and LLM recommendations.

It’s similar to SEO, but the rules are different: the AI evaluates your content for helpfulness, clarity, and relevance when generating answers.

LUMO will optimize your SEO and further build upon it for GEO performance as well.
